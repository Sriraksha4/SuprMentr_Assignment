1. College – Student Performance Prediction
Machine learning can be used in colleges to predict student academic performance.

Input: Attendance, study hours, internal marks, assignment scores
Output: Predicted final exam marks or pass/fail result

This can help teachers identify students who may need extra support.

2. Healthcare – Disease Prediction
Machine learning can help doctors predict diseases at an early stage.

Input: Age, symptoms, blood pressure, sugar levels, medical history
Output: Predicted disease risk or possible health condition

This can improve early diagnosis and treatment planning.

3. Shopping – Product Recommendation System
Machine learning can improve online shopping by suggesting products.

Input: Search history, clicked products, purchase history, ratings
Output: Recommended products

This helps users find items they may be interested in buying.

4. Transportation – Traffic Prediction
Machine learning can be used to predict traffic and congestion levels.

Input: Time, day, weather, road usage data
Output: Predicted traffic level or best route suggestion

This helps save travel time and reduce road congestion.
