1. Google Maps
Google Maps uses AI to predict traffic and suggest the fastest routes. Machine learning studies past traffic patterns and live location data to improve route suggestions.

2. YouTube Recommendations
YouTube uses AI to recommend videos based on what I watch, like, and search. ML identifies similar viewing behavior among users and suggests relevant content.

3. Instagram Feed
Instagram uses AI to decide which posts and reels to show first. Machine learning analyzes likes, shares, comments, and watch time to rank content.

4. Gmail Spam Filter
Gmail uses AI to detect spam emails automatically. ML models are trained on thousands of spam and genuine emails to classify incoming messages.

5. Netflix Recommendations
Netflix uses AI to suggest movies and web series based on my watch history. ML compares my interests with similar users and predicts what I may like.

6. Amazon Product Suggestions
Amazon uses AI to recommend products while shopping. Machine learning studies browsing history, purchases, and product clicks to make suggestions.

7. Voice Assistants (Siri / Alexa / Google Assistant)
These assistants use AI to understand voice commands and reply. NLP (Natural Language Processing) helps convert speech into meaningful actions.

8. Google Search
Google Search uses AI to rank the most useful results. ML understands user intent and improves search relevance based on patterns and previous searches.

9. Face Unlock on Smartphone
Face unlock uses AI and computer vision to recognize my face. ML helps compare facial features and identify whether the face matches the authorized user.
